TickrIsland Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how TickrIsland ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and disposes of your personal information.
1. Information We Collect
Automatically Collected

Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only when ATT permission is granted
APNs Push Token — for delivering Live Activity updates
Device Information — model, iOS version, app version, language settings
Ad Interaction Data — impressions and clicks (Google AdMob)
Diagnostic Data — crash logs and performance metrics

The App does not require sign-up or login and does not collect identifying information such as name, email, phone number, or wallet address. The App does not require any user-entered data; selected watchlist items are stored only on the device.
2. Purpose of Processing

Display real-time price data via Live Activity / Dynamic Island
Serve advertisements and measure ad performance
Improve app stability and diagnose errors

3. Retention Period

IDFA: deleted immediately upon ATT permission revocation or app deletion
APNs Token: until Live Activity ends or the token expires (APNs 410 Gone)
Ad and diagnostic data: governed by each processor's policy (Google, Apple)

4. Third-Party Disclosure
The App does not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Limited data is processed only through the service providers listed below.
5. Service Providers
ProviderPurposeData ProcessedGoogle AdMobAd serving and measurementIDFA, ad interactions, device infoFly.io (US)Backend hostingAPNs tokenApple APNsPush notification deliveryAPNs token
Provider policies: Google · Fly.io · Apple
International transfers: Fly.io servers are located in US regions. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS.
6. Price Data Source
Price data is sourced from the Hyperliquid Public WebSocket API. This source provides public market data, and no user personal information is transmitted to it.
The App is not the official Hyperliquid app and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Hyperliquid Labs.
7. Your Rights
You may exercise the following rights at any time:

Revoke ATT permission: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
Delete all on-device data: uninstall the App
Request server-side APNs token deletion: contact the email below

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under applicable law (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, PIPA), including access, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
8. Data Disposal
Upon expiration of the retention period or completion of the processing purpose, data is permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods.
9. Security Measures

Technical: TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit
Administrative: minimized access privileges, separation of operational environments

10. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 14 (or 13 under COPPA, where applicable) and does not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.
11. Disclaimer
Price data shown in the App is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice or a solicitation to trade. We make no warranty as to the accuracy, timeliness, or completeness of the data. You bear sole responsibility for any decisions made based on this information. The App does not offer trading, transfer, or wallet functionality.
